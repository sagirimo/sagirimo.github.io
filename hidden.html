A Hidden Corner

This is a private space. Not indexed. Not shared. Just thoughts.

On Freedom

Freedom is not license, but the capacity for self-determination. It is one's control over their own destiny, independence of thought, and the right to expression.

True freedom requires courage—the courage to question, to bear consequences, to endure solitude.

On Power

Power requires constraint. Absolute power corrupts absolutely. This is not political theory, but human nature.

"When power goes unchecked, people lose their dignity."

On the Future

I hope one day, these words can be written openly under the sun, not hidden in a corner of code.

When that day comes, it will be through the efforts of countless people—including my own.

On Choice

Everyone has the right to choose their stance. Some choose silence, some conformity, some to leave, some to resist.

I choose to record, to think, to speak when it becomes safe to do so. This is not cowardice, but waiting for the right moment. To live itself is resistance.
