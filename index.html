2026.3 – Present Independent Research

Breast Tumor Localization: Prone MRI → Supine Prediction

Medical Physics & Image Processing · Shenzhen No.2 Hospital

Predicting supine breast tumor position from prone MRI using surface point cloud registration and PointNet++. A novel clinical pipeline combining ICP rigid alignment, CPD non-rigid deformation, and deep learning. 314 paired cases from Shenzhen No.2 Hospital — a rare dataset size in this domain. Targeting JBHI 2026. First author.
